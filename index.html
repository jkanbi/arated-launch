The Story...

The 'A rated' story started almost 20 years ago, when a young person was in their teenage years and searching for a job, but unfortunately the businesses kept saying you don't have enough experience or skills.

Fast forward to 2015, the world has changed. It is now very technology driven and mobile oriented. Most jobs now require technology skills of some sort. That young person now has a host of technology and business skills, after a variety of jobs and failed startups. That person has a passion for technology for people.

Unfortunately, now the businesses are now saying, you do not fit our criteria, you are too scattered, possess too much diverse skills and not enough focus. This is company talk for, you do not fit into our slots and your diversity has no place in our archaic profit centered ecosystem.

Well it's time for a change.
